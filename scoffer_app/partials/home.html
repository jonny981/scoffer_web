What is Scoffer?

Scoffer is a daily deals company that is completely free to the consumer and business.

It is simply a means of advertising, it requires no monetary transactions and takes no royalties when a customer redeems an offer. The mission statement is to provide everyone with a means of seeing the best offers in their current location at the current time.

Scoffer consists of a website for business use, and an Android application for consumer use.

Note: This is a beta version of scoffer - please be patient while we add new features and fix any bugs that may have persisted!

How does it work?

Scoffer is designed to be a little different than the average daily deals company.

The sole purpose is to show the consumer what offers are near their current location, at the time they are there. The aim is to tap into the consumer's spontaneity, sometimes it can be cumbersome to pre-organise something as simple as redeeming an offer for a cup of coffee with some daily deals sites. With this process often involving online or in-app purchases as well as the need to show a verification email or printout on redemption. To combat this Scoffer has been made as simple to use as possible:

For a business - simply create an account and start posting... as many offers as you like!

For a consumer - download the android app and start saving money! No account necessary!

Who Can Use Scoffer?

Scoffer is limited only to offers from companies within food and drink industry. If any offers are posted regarding another industry, or if they are deemed to be spam, then they will be promptly removed. If a user prompts an inappropriate offer then they will blocked from posting any further offers.
